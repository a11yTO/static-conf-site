You've asked, and we've listened! Opportunity tickets are here! Thanks to the generosity of our sponsors, we are able to offer a limited number of deeply discounted ($99) tickets to #a11yTO Conf this year.

Students, and/or those facing financial barriers are encouraged to submit an application to take advantage of this initiative. A limited number of tickets will be available for both in-person and online attendance

Applications will be accepted until July 1st, 2024. All applicants will be notified by July 12th, 2024 so those not selected can still take advantage of Early Bird pricing.

Please note:

  • Opportunity tickets are for conference admission only. We do not subsidize travel or accommodation
  • You may be offered an in-person OR online ticket

Opportunity Ticket Application submissions closed on July 1st.

If you have any questions, please contact us at info@a11yto.com.
