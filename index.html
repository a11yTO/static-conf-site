The Event

For the first time, #a11yTO Conf will be 100% virtual! From Wednesday, October 21st to Friday, October 23rd, we will host three days of virtual accessibility training, with speakers from around the world!

Our unique three-day, online track conference will be a curated "playlist" of lectures, demos, and lightning talks.

Our mix of presentations and learning styles provides a unique and immersive experience. #a11yTO Conf will run from 10 am to 3 pm ET, with a break for lunch each day.

You Can Still Watch

Tickets are still on sale. Get your tickets and catch what you missed until November 30th.

If you already have tickets from this years Conf and missed sessions or would like to watch something again, visit a11yTO.tv to access the videos.
