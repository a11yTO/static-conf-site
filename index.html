#a11yTOConf

October 15 and 16, 2018

Toronto, Ontario

A curated playlist of talks, focused on digital accessibility

The Event

On October 15 and 16 we will open the doors at Telus Digital, in downtown Toronto, to over 300 attendees for two full days of accessibility training!

Our 2-day, 1-track conference will be comprised of a carefully curated "playlist " of lectures, demos, lightning talks, and workshops.

Our mix of presentations and learning styles will provide a unique and immersive learning experience that differs from our yearly camp while holding ourselves to the level of quality attendees have grown to expect from the #a11yTO brand.

We're also hoping to introduce other special events throughout the week leading up to the conference. These would include activities such as guided tours of local museums, galleries, and workshops.

Don't take our word for it, see for yourself in this awesome recap of last year from our friends at Accessible Media Inc!
