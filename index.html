The Event

For the first time, #a11yTO Conf will be 100% virtual!

From Wednesday, October 21st to Friday, October 23rd, we will host three days of virtual accessibility training, with speakers from around the world!

Our unique three-day, online track conference will be a curated "playlist" of lectures, demos, and lightning talks.

Our mix of presentations and learning styles provides a unique and immersive experience.
