Who is #a11yTO?

#a11yTO has been creating, supporting, and expanding the digital accessibility community in Toronto since 2011. #a11yTO provides digital accessibility knowledge to curious humans through meetups, camps, and conferences.

What is #a11yTO Conf?

#a11yTO Conf is the only event of its kind in Canada. Held in Toronto, #a11yTO Conf is a two-day, single-track digital accessibility conference.

Our event has reached capacity every year, attracting over 400 people annually from all over the world. Past playlists have included curated world-class speakers from Australia, Japan, Austria, Netherlands, United Kingdom, United States, and Canada. #a11yTO Conf is an established destination event that accessibility professionals plan on attending every year. 

#a11yTO Conf is one of a few programs recognized by the International Association of Accessibility Professionals (IAAP) for its Continuing Accessibility Education Credit. 

What topics are covered?

This year’s event will cover many topics, including:

  • Inclusive design and Cognitive Disability
  • Accessible eLearning
  • Practical lessons for improving text resizing
  • Using the accessibility tree to debug HTML
  • Accessibility for young coders
  • Building an accessibility knowledge hub
  • Accessibility conformance reports and vendor monitoring programs
  • Accessibility overlays through a scientific lens
  • Inclusive digital geographic mapping
  • Mobile app accessibility
  • Usability testing
  • Leveraging CMS to create more accessible digital experiences
