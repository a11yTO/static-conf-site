Our initial speaker line-up announcement for 2025 Conf.

Quintin Balsdon

Quintin Balsdon has a background in Android development. He fell in love with Accessibility in 2020 and loves playing with tech to make things more accessible.

Quintin likes all things native mobile, the Internet of Things, test driven development and automation.

Pooja Joshi

Pooja Joshi is a writer, producer, and stand-up comic who thrives on telling bold, people-focused stories.

She has performed for 500-strong crowds, trained an ensemble cast of 20 actors on a feature film, and jumped from 20,000 feet—because why not?

Pooja's work has been showcased at the South Asian Film Festival in New York, and her global journey across Africa, India, and Canada fuels her storytelling.

With a film degree from York University and drama training in Toronto and Mumbai, She brings a fearless, resourceful, and can-do mindset to every project—always seeking the story in the unexpected.
